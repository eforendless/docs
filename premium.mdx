## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I need premium to use the bot?">
    No. Moderation and verification features are available without premium. Premium is only required for AI chat (/viai) and image generation (/generate).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How are tokens calculated?">
    Tokens measure AI usage. Text responses consume up to your per-request cap. Images deduct a flat cost per generation based on your tier.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I share premium across servers?">
    Your premium tier is tied to your account for AI usage. Server-wide benefits may be offered in the future.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Where can I buy premium?">
